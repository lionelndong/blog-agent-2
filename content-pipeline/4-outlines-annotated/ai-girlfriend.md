**Target:** ~700 words (longest section)
**BLUF:** Most platform comparisons focus on the wrong things. What predicts whether you'll still be using a platform a month from now is memory, character coherence, image quality, voice integration, friction, and uncensored honesty.

**Key points (each becomes a sub-thread):**

1. **Memory across long conversations** — test by referencing something specific from 3 sessions ago
