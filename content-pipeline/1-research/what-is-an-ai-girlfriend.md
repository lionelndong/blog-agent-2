## Recommended angle

**Thesis:** *An AI girlfriend is a custom-built character running on a large language model with persistent memory and a chosen persona — and the more useful question isn't "what is it?" but "what's the experience actually like, and is it for you?"*

**Why this angle wins:** Every informational page on the current SERP either over-indexes on technology (no soul) or over-indexes on mental-health concern-trolling (no texture). The product pages skip definition entirely. None deliver the candid, definition-plus-lived-experience read the searcher is actually looking for. Pleasur.AI's voice — practical, direct, conversational, comfortable with the adult context — is uniquely positioned to deliver that without flinching, then point readers to a sensible starting place at the end.
